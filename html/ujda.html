Synopsis

The story revolves around Chaman Kohli (Sunny Singh), a 30-year old bachelor suffering from premature baldness and in search of a beautiful wife. Upon getting rejected several times as a groom because of his appearance, when he is given a deadline by an astrologer to find the suitable match or remain a celibate forever, it leads Chaman to an amusing roller-coaster ride of self-acceptance.
