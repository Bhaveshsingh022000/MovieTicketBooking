Synopsis

Inspired by the real-life sharpshooters, the film chronicles the lives of Chandro Tomar (Shooter Dadi) and her sister-in-law Prakashi Tomar (Revolver Dadi). A biographical drama set against the backdrop of Uttar Pradesh, it features Bhumi Pednekar and Taapsee Pannu in the lead roles of oldest sharpshooters of India.
